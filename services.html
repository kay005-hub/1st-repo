Humber North Campus is the largest campus of Humber Polytechnic (formerly Humber College) and is located in northwest Toronto

It is adjacent to the Humber River and 100 acres of parkland.The official address is 205 Humber College Blvd.
Toronto, ON M9W 5L7. Humber North Campus is a modern, full-service educational environment offering extensive programs, state-of-the-art facilities, and a strong sense of community in a natural setting within Toronto.

GYMNASIUM

The Humber North Campus Gymnasium is a state-of-the-art facility supporting a wide range of athletic and community events. It offers extensive sports courts, seating, and fitness equipment, making it a central hub for physical activity and large-scale events on campus. It has 8 Basketball Hoops, 10 Badminton Courts, Suitable for basketball, volleyball and other indoor sports. It contains main bleachers with 1,174 numbered seats and four additional bleachers units with 120 seats each etc. It is located at 205 Humber college Blvd, Toronto,ON M9W 5L7.
